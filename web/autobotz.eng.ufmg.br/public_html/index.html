Autobotz is a research and competition group from the Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG) focused on mechatronics, control, and robotics. Our objective is to participate in competitions and push science forward.

Mostra de profissões 2017

Autobotz was in the Mostra de Profissões 2017 UFMG. The members prepared robots and other Autobot’s experiments to arouse the interest of highschool students in engineering and science. The exposition lasted the whole day and students were able to interact with the projects and talk with the team.
